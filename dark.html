dark

The series Dark is a German show about a small town where children mysteriously disappear. During the investigation, people discover a cave that allows time travel. As the story develops, it is revealed that four families are deeply connected across the past, present, and future, and everything that happens in one time period affects the others. The show explores the idea that time is not a straight line but a repeating cycle, where every action changes the fate of the characters in complex and mysterious ways.
